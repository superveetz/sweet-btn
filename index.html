Sweet Button Demo

A jQuery plugin that gives you options for how your buttons should look and feel.

How It Works

  • Step 1. Add sweet-btn and sweet-btn-{size} class names to any element:

    • - {size} options:
    • xl
    • lg
    • md
    • sm
    • xs

    Ex. <i class='sweet-btn sweet-btn-xl'></i>

  • Step 2. Add sweet-btn-{attribute-name}={value} attributes to any element:

    - There are currently 104 different attributes that can be supported simultaneously so we will divide the {attribute-name} options into two categories:

    • Appearance
    • &
    • Events

Appearance

  • Shape Attribute: sweet-btn-shape-{#}={type}

    • - {#} options:
    • 1
    • 2
    • 3
    • 4
    • 5
    Note: The {#} indicates the z-index or the stacking order for multiple shapes.
    • - {type} options:
    • hexagon
    • square
    • circle

    Ex. <i class='sweet-btn sweet-btn-xs'
                sweet-btn-shape-4='hexagon'></i>
